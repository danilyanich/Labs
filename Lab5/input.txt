*Wikipedia* is a free online encyclopedia that *allows* its users to edit almost any article.

Sanger *coined its* name, a *portmanteau* of wiki and encyclopedia. It was only in the English language initially,
but it quickly***developed similar versions *in other languages** which differ in content and in editing practices.
With 5,265,049 articles, English Wikipedia is the largest out of more than 290 versions of encyclopedias on Wikipedia.
Overall, Wikipedia consists of more than 40 *million articles in more* than* **250 different languages
and as of February 2*014*, it had 18 billion *page* views and *nearly* 500 million unique visitors each month.
